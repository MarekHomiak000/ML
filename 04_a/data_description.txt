url – Link to the individual car listing.

region – The city or metropolitan area where the car is listed.

region_url – URL of the Craigslist regional site for the listing.

price – Asking price of the car (in USD).

year – Model year of the car.

manufacturer – Brand or maker of the car (e.g., Toyota, Ford).

model – Specific model of the car (e.g., Camry, F-150).

condition – Seller’s assessment of the car’s condition 
            - excellent
            - good
            - fair
            - like new
            - new    
            - salvage                 

cylinders – Number of engine cylinders (numeric, e.g., 4, 6, 8).

fuel – Type of fuel used by the car 
        - gas
        - other
        - diesel
        - electric
        - hybrid        

odometer – Mileage of the car (in miles).

title_status – Legal status of the car’s title        
                - clean         
                - rebuilt         
                - salvage         
                - lien            
                - missing          
                - parts only       

transmission – Type of gearbox
                - automatic    
                - other         
                - manual         

drive – Drivetrain configuration 
            - 4wd
            - fwd
            - rwd

size – Size classification of the car 
        - compact 
        - mid-size 
        - full-size 
        - sub-compact

type – Vehicle type
        - sedan          
        - SUV            
        - pickup         
        - truck          
        - other          
        - coupe          
        - hatchback      
        - wagon          
        - van             
        - convertible     
        - mini-van        
        - offroad          
        - bus              
        

paint_color – Exterior color of the car.

image_url – URL of the listing’s main image.

description – Text description provided by the seller.

county – County where the car is located.

state – Two-letter U.S. state code (e.g., CA, NY, TX).

lat – Latitude coordinate of the car’s location.

long – Longitude coordinate of the car’s location.

posting_date – Date the listing was posted.
