1.  Age Description: Age of the customer in years.

2.  Gender Description: Gender identity of the customer (e.g., Male,
    Female).

3.  Marital Status Description: Current marital status of the customer
    (Single, Married, Divorced, Widowed, Separated).

4.  Education Level Description: Highest completed education level
    (e.g., Associate Degree, Bachelor’s Degree, Doctorate).

5.  Geographic Information Description: State or region where the
    customer resides.

6.  Occupation Description: The customer’s primary profession or job
    role.

7.  Income Level Description: Annual income of the customer (numeric
    value).

8.  Behavioral Data Description: Internal behavioral segment or customer
    behavior category used by the company.

9.  Purchase History Description: Date when the customer purchased the
    policy or product.

10. Interactions with Customer Service Description: The communication
    channel used by the customer when interacting with customer support
    (Phone, Chat, Email).

11. Insurance Products Owned Description: Identifier of the insurance
    policy owned by the customer.

12. Coverage Amount Description: The total coverage value provided by
    the insurance policy.

13. Premium Amount Description: The periodic payment amount the customer
    pays for the insurance policy.

14. Policy Type Description: Category of the insurance policy (e.g.,
    Group, Family).

15. Customer Preferences Description: Customer’s preferred method for
    receiving communications or offers.

16. Preferred Communication Channel Description: The main channel the
    customer prefers for communication (Email, Mail, Text, etc.).

17. Preferred Contact Time Description: The time period when the
    customer prefers to be contacted (Morning, Afternoon, Evening,
    Weekends, Anytime).

18. Preferred Language Description: The language the customer prefers
    for communication.
